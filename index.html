Associate Professor at the Institute of Maintenance and Industrial Safety, affiliated with the Department of Industrial Engineering at the University of Oran 2 Mohamed Ben Ahmed, and the Laboratory of Nonlinear Analysis and Applied Mathematics at the University of Tlemcen, Algeria.

My research focuses on mathematical modeling, particularly in:

  • Differential equations
  • Dynamical systems
  • Applied mathematics

I have published work on topics such as:

  • The stability of neutral type systems
  • Properties of neutral delay differential equations
  • Mathematical modeling in fisheries and tumor-immune systems

My current research explores delay equations, mathematical modeling, and fractional calculus.
